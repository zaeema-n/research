# Meetings Registry

Aggregated view of all statutory body meeting requirements across acts under the Ministry of Education. This registry grows automatically as more acts are analyzed in depth.

:::note Regulation-Defined Details
Unlike the Health Ministry's statutory bodies where meeting requirements are often specified directly in the Act, the Public Examinations Act delegates advisory committee constitution — including meeting rules — to **ministerial regulations** under Section 22(2)(e). Many fields below reference these regulations rather than specific Act sections.
:::
